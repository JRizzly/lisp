The Importance of Trimming the Trees on One's Property

Many people love the trees they have dispersed in their landscape. Yet, they dread the maintenance such yard fixtures require. Tree trimming calls for more work, more expenses, and more time wasted. Though maintaining a stunning scenery can at times be a bother and many people do not like to take care of it, Lawn In Order has encountered numerous reasons that confirm the importance of trimming the trees on one's property:

  • Dead wood
    • Not only do dead branches and lifeless twigs cause an eye sore, they can also fall from the tree and make a mess of one's lawn, making it even more difficult to mow and maintain.
  • Tree is blocking too much light
    • This is bad for two reasons. First, as is commonly known, a yard needs sufficient sunlight to grow healthily. Second, trees can block any natural sunlight from entering one's home. Proper thinning out and pruning of trees in a yard reduces such inconveniences.
  • Branches are interfering with your activities and views around driveways, doorways, windows, patios, pool and the like
    • Clearing out branches can make your entire property less cluttered and more enjoyable all around. Trees hanging too low over a driveway or parking lot tend to get in the way. More importantly, by law, trees must not hang too far into streets or other public areas.
  • Weak or diseased branches
    • Removal of weak or diseased branches must be performed properly in order to prevent spreading of these problems and to promote a healthy tree.
  • Aesthetics
    • Proper thinning out and pruning simply takes a yard from unsightly to beautiful. The benefits include coming home to a luscious landscape every day and adding value to a home when it goes on sale.
  • Safety for people, children, cars, sheds, play structures, pets, and small trees or shrubs growing underneath
    • Lawn In Order regards this as the most important reason to have trees properly trimmed. Falling branches can cause expensive damages to prized possessions or even inflict priceless injuries to loved ones.
